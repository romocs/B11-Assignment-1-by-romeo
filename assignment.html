Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has shown significant progress in fostering innovation, particularly in digital transformation, mobile banking (bKash), and tech startups, driving economic growth and service accessibility.

Sustainability

Efforts in sustainability focus on renewable energy, eco-friendly garment production, and climate resilience projects to combat vulnerabilities from natural disasters and environmental challenges.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure development, including the Padma Bridge, metro rail, and deep-sea ports, has enhanced connectivity, trade, and overall economic growth.

Empowerment

Programs for women’s empowerment, microfinance initiatives, and skill development projects have uplifted marginalized groups, contributing to social and economic inclusion.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

They have no control over capital, and it is the ability to control capital that gives people the power to rise out of poverty.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus
